An AI Powered Tool where users can upload their resume and get ATS score , receive Keyword suggestions based on a Job description, improve bullet points using AI
AI Resume Analyzer & Optimizer is powerful tool designed to bridge the gap between job seekers and the Applicant Tracking system (ATS) used by recruiters.
##Key features 
**ATS Score Calculation:** Instantly get a compatibility percentage matching your resume against a target job description
**Keyword Gap Analaysis:**identify crucial industry keywords and skills missing from your resume.

**Section - by - Section Feedback:* Receive tailored recommendations on formatting, impact phrasing and structure.
